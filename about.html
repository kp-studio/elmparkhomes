About us - Elm Park Homes
About us

Elm Park Homes was established in 2004 with a vision of providing new homes with great design, excellent build quality and high specification. The company is privately owned and prioritise quality and longevity over quick profits to ensure that our developments stand the test of time.

Our homes are traditionally built but provide the convenience of practically and low maintenance demanded by today's modern lifestyles.

We provide a first class service allowing customers to individually design aspects of their own home from tile and kitchen choices, through to bespoke extras where available.

We are serious about customer service and always aim to be professional, efficient and helpful and are happy to offer advice to help you make the right choice, for you.
